Children's Counting Book

June 2025

I had the opportunity to tutor three wonderful kids in my last quarter at Stanford through the Haas Center's Ravenswood Reeds and Preschool Counts programs. One of my kids was also graduating preschool at the start of June, so I made this book as a graduation gift, inspired by the storytelling and counting games we were taught in class. It follows a little goose on the day of their own preschool graduation!
